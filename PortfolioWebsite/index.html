Since I was little, I have always been fascinated with the inner workings of technology, and that fascination led me to my current passion of software development. Check out my projects.

Naturally, because I think about the inner workings of technology, I often think about the inner workings of my mind and how it thinks. "How can I live my life to the fullest?" I successfully managed a website where I sold the clothes and got amazing support from friends and family. Along with the brand, Happenings, came its motto, Live a Little, which means to stop worrying about the things you can't control, and focus on the things you can control. Although I am not currently working on the brand at the moment, I plan on returning to it one day to continue spreading that message.
